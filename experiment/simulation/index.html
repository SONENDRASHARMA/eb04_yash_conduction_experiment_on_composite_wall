Thermal conductivity of MILD STEEL : K1

K1=Q * L1/A*(T1-T2)

Themal of conductivity of BAKELITE : K2

K2=Q * L2/A*(T2-T3)

Themal of conductivity of WOOD : K3

K3=Q * L3/A*(T3-T4)

A : Area of Cross-Section

A = (3.14 * D^2 )/4

D : Diameter of Plates

L : Thickness of Disc

  • L1 :Thickness of Mild steel

  • L2 :Thickness of Bakelite

  • L3 :Thickness of Wood
